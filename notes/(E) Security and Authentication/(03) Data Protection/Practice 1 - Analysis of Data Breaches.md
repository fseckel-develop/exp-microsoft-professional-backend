## Objective

Learn to identify and analyse real-world data protection breaches and understand their impact on confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA Triad).

---
## Example 1: Equifax Data Breach (2017)

### Scenario Overview

- Major credit reporting agency compromised
- Web application vulnerability exploited
- Data exposed:
	- Social Security numbers
	- Birthdates
	- Addresses
- Affected:
	- ~147 million individuals

### CIA Triad Impact Analysis

- Confidentiality:
	- Sensitive personal data exposed
- Integrity:
	- Potential risk of altered credit records
- Availability:
	- No major service disruption reported

### Impact on Users and Company

- Users:
	- Increased risk of identity theft
	- Potential financial fraud
- Company:
	- Legal action and regulatory fines
	- Severe reputational damage

### Prevention Measures

- Apply security patches promptly
- Perform regular security audits and penetration testing
- Encrypt sensitive personal data

---
## Example 2: Colonial Pipeline Ransomware Attack (2021)

### Scenario Overview

- Ransomware attack on critical infrastructure
- IT systems encrypted by attackers
- Fuel distribution halted across Eastern U.S.
- Ransom demanded for system restoration

### CIA Triad Impact Analysis

- Confidentiality:
	- No confirmed sensitive data exposure
- Integrity:
	- Risk of compromised operational systems
- Availability:
	- Pipeline operations halted for several days

### Impact on Users and Company

- Users:
	- Fuel shortages and price spikes
	- Disruption to transportation and businesses
- Company:
	- Paid $4.4 million ransom
	- Reputational and regulatory consequences

### Prevention Measures

- Network segmentation for critical systems
- Regular backups and tested recovery plans
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)

## Example 3: Capital One Data Breach (2019)

### Scenario Overview

- Cloud-based data breach
- Misconfigured firewall exploited
- Data exposed:
	- Credit card applications
	- Social Security numbers
- Affected:
	- Over 100 million customers

### CIA Triad Impact Analysis

- Confidentiality:
	- Sensitive customer information exposed
- Integrity:
	- No confirmed data tampering
- Availability:
	- Services largely unaffected

### Impact on Users and Company

- Users:
	- Increased risk of identity theft and fraud
- Company:
	- Lawsuits and regulatory fines
	- Damage to brand trust

### Prevention Measures

- Audit and monitor cloud configurations
- Use automated tools to detect misconfigurations
- Encrypt sensitive cloud-stored data

---
## Example 4: Healthcare Ransomware Attack (2020)

### Scenario Overview

- Major hospital system targeted
- Electronic Medical Records (EMRs) encrypted
- Patient care delayed due to system outages
- Ransom demanded for decryption

### CIA Triad Impact Analysis

- Confidentiality:
	- No confirmed data leakage
- Integrity:
	- Risk of altered or corrupted medical records
- Availability:
	- EMRs and critical systems unavailable

### Impact on Patients and Provider

- Patients:
	- Delayed treatments and increased health risks
- Healthcare Provider:
	- Operational disruption
	- Financial loss and reputational damage

### Prevention Measures

- Regular EMR backups with tested recovery plans
- Endpoint protection and anti-ransomware tools
- Staff training on phishing and social engineering

---
## Key Takeaways

- Data breaches often impact multiple CIA principles
- Confidentiality is most commonly affected in data leaks
- Availability is a primary target in ransomware attacks
- Strong security practices reduce both likelihood and impact of breaches
